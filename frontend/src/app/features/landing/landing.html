The problem

In Nigeria, bank transfer is how people pay. But when hundreds of customers pay into one account, matching each payment to the right person is manual, slow, and error-prone — businesses read transfer narrations, match amounts, and call payers just to work out who paid.

The solution

Give every customer their own dedicated account number, and the account a payment lands on tells you exactly who paid — reconciliation becomes automatic. Kolekta handles the hard part: HMAC-verified webhooks, exactly-once accounting on a double-entry ledger, and a resolution queue for anything that doesn't cleanly match.

What you get

Dedicated accounts

Exactly-once reconciliation

Every transfer matched to the right customer — no double-counting.
